# Staffing (SME operations)

> Public, challenge-safe placeholder notes. No customer or private data.

## Concept

Most small operators rely on a very small number of trusted staff. Coverage,
delegation, and trust are the real constraints — not headcount. Expansion
multiplies staffing risk because trusted people do not scale linearly.

## What to check (triage)

- Who can run the operation if the owner is absent?
- Which tasks are concentrated in one trusted person?
- What is the coverage plan during peak hours / market days?
- What delegation is safe today vs. what requires the owner?

## What to avoid

- Assuming one trusted staff member can cover two locations.
- Delegating cash handling without any informal checkpoint.
- Expanding before a second trusted person exists.
