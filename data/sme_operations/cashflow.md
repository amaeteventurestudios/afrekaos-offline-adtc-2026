# Cashflow (SME operations)

> Public, challenge-safe placeholder notes. No customer or private data.

## Concept

Cashflow pressure is about timing, not just totals. An operator can be
profitable on paper and still unable to restock or pay staff because cash is
tied up in stock, credit extended to customers, or pending supplier payments.

## What to check (triage)

- Cash on hand vs. immediate obligations (restock, staff, rent).
- How much cash is tied up in customer credit.
- When the next supplier payment is due vs. when cash arrives.
- Which expenses are fixed vs. variable.

## What to avoid

- Extending more credit when cash is already thin.
- Committing to new fixed costs before confirming cash timing.
- Treating irregular cash records as if they were complete.
