# Credit (SME operations)

> Public, challenge-safe placeholder notes. No customer or private data.

## Concept

Customer credit is common in African SME operations and is a major lever for
both loyalty and risk. The decision is rarely "credit or no credit" — it is
about who, how much, and when to refuse.

## What to check (triage)

- Customer's repayment history (informal records count).
- Share of total cash currently tied up in credit.
- Whether the request is for a fast-moving or slow-moving item.
- Whether refusing now damages a relationship that matters.

## What to avoid

- Granting credit to everyone to keep the peace when cash is thin.
- Extending credit without any informal record of who owes what.
- Using credit to mask an underlying sales or stock problem.
