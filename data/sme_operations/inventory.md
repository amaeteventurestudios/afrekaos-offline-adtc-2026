# Inventory (SME operations)

> Public, challenge-safe placeholder notes. No customer or private data.

## Concept

Inventory is the most visible operational pressure point for a small shop or
market operator. Stockouts of fast-moving items directly cost sales and trust;
overstock ties up limited working capital.

## What to check (triage)

- Which items are fast-moving vs. slow-moving?
- Current stock level of each fast-moving item vs. typical daily/weekly run-rate.
- Reorder lead time per supplier.
- Which stockouts are driving the sales dip right now?

## What to avoid

- Panic-reordering slow-moving items.
- Tying up cash in bulk buys of items with thin margins.
- Ignoring the difference between "out of stock" and "not selling".
