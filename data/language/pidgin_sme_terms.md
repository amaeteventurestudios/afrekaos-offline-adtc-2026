# Nigerian Pidgin SME Terms — AfrekaOS Offline

> Public, challenge-safe glossary. Simple and respectful — no comic exaggeration.

## Common SME operations terms (Nigerian Pidgin)

- **Inventory / Goods wey you get** — the goods wey dey shop for sale.
- **Supplier / Person wey dey supply goods** — the person or company wey dey bring goods.
- **Customer / Person wey dey buy** — the person wey dey buy from you.
- **Credit / Sell on credit ("owo")** — make person carry goods now, e go pay later.
- **Cashflow / How money dey move** — how money dey enter and comot for business.
- **Records / Your book / how you write am** — wetin you write for sales, stock, and money.
- **Stockout / When market goods finish** — when the fast-moving goods finish and no more dey.
- **Expansion / Make business bigger** — carry the business go new place or make am bigger.
- **Staff / People wey work for you** — the people wey dey work for the business.
- **Daily checklist / Wetin to do every day** — short list of work to do every day.
