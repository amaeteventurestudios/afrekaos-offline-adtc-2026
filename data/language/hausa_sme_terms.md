# Hausa SME Terms — AfrekaOS Offline

> Public, challenge-safe glossary. No private data.

## Common SME operations terms (Hausa)

- **Inventory / Kayayyaki** — kayan da ake siyarwa a shago.
- **Supplier / Mai bayar da kaya** — wanda yake kawo kayan zuwa shago.
- **Customer / Abokin ciniki** — wanda yake siyan kaya.
- **Credit / Lada / Bashin ciniki** — bawa abokin ciniki kayi yanzu ya biya daga baya.
- **Cashflow / Gudanar da kuɗi** — yadda kuɗi ke shiga da fita a cikin kasuwa.
- **Records / Bayanan aiki** — rubutun siyarwa, kaya, da kuɗi.
- **Stockout / Ƙarewar kaya** — saurarin kaya suka gama ba a samu ba.
- **Expansion / Faɗaɗa kasuwa** — ƙara girman kasuwa ko buɗe sabon wuri.
- **Staff / Ma'aikata** — mutanen da ke aiki a cikin kasuwa.
- **Daily checklist / Jerin ayyukan yau** — gajeren jerin ayyukan da ake yi kullum.
