# English SME Terms — AfrekaOS Offline

> Public, challenge-safe glossary. No private data. No customer data.

## Common SME operations terms

- **Inventory** — the goods a shop holds to sell.
- **Supplier** — the business or person that delivers goods to the shop.
- **Customer** — the person who buys from the shop.
- **Credit** — allowing a customer to take goods now and pay later.
- **Cashflow** — the movement of cash in and out of the business.
- **Records** — written or digital notes of sales, stock, and money.
- **Stockout** — when a fast-moving item finishes and there is none to sell.
- **Expansion** — growing the business to a new location or larger operation.
- **Staff** — the people who work in the business.
- **Daily checklist** — a short list of operating tasks to do each day.
